About Us


We are a wellness center which offers meditation retreats, beauty and spa services. Located within the picturesque tranquil valley of the Magalies mountains, immediately the calmness of the mountain and the music from the local birds, envelops you. We are a home of many bird species which adds to the calmness of this place. We intentionally have no cellphone service or reception. Our nearest town is about 30KM.
